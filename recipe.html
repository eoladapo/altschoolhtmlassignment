This flavor-packed stir fry spaghetti combines tender pasta, colorful vegetables, and your choice of protein, tossed in a savory stir fry sauce. A quick and healthy meal for busy weeknights!

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, green)
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked chicken strips or beef slices
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant, then add bell peppers and carrots. Stir fry for 3‚Äì4 minutes.
  4. Add cooked chicken or beef and stir until heated through.
  5. Stir in the spaghetti, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Toss well to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, cook for another 2 minutes, and serve hot.

Tips

For a vegetarian option, skip the meat and add mushrooms or tofu. You can also use chili sauce for a spicy twist.
